About the Pharmacy

Quality medicines, stocked with care

Akash Medicine House sits in the heart of Daya Muhallah, Kandhkot, stocked for the conditions families here actually manage day to day — heart disease, diabetes and blood pressure — alongside the everyday medicines every household needs.

Beyond medicines, we keep the devices that go with long-term care: BP monitors, glucometers, nebulizers and more, with the counter staff to explain how to use them. We serve individual customers across the counter, and supply shops, clinics and other pharmacies on a wholesale basis.

Why families choose us

  • Genuine medicines, checked for expiry every time
  • Counter staff who explain how to use your devices
  • A direct line to the pharmacist for any question
